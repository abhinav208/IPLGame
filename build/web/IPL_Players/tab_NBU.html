NBU

  • NBU - Non Bidding User
  • User who don't bid in match, 50 points will be deducted as NBU-FINE
  • Total NBU-FINE will be distributed between user who have bid on match
    Ex: If there are total 5 user don't Bid on match
    then total NBU-FILE will be 5 x 50 = 250 points
    there are total 10 users who have bid on this match, then 250 points will be distributed in these 10 users as NBU-BENEFITS (i.e 25 points)
